Stage 7: Warrington to Liverpool

Our suggested route between Warrington and Liverpool is to leave Warrington along the Manchester Ship Canal where the path is direct and easy to follow, then crossing the River mersey at Runcorn to Widenes before following the river for a while before heading through Hale Bank and running alongside the A562 towards Allerton, then suburban roads to Aigburth and the A561 all the way to the Baltic Triangle and the Liverpool Docks where there are a variety of taprooms.

This route is approximately 35.5km with a total climb of 107m.
