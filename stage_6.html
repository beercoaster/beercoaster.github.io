Stage 6: Manchester to Warrington

For the Manchester to Warrington route we recommend making full use of the Bridgewater Canal.

Leaving Manchester City Centre through the University district and the long, straight and easy to follow roads of Stretford Road and Talbot Road past Old Trafford Cricket Ground before joining the Bridgewater Canal towpath at Stretford.

The well maintained towpath along the Bridgewater Canal will take runners out of Manchester and to the other side of Altrincham where they can then join the Trans Penine Trail right through to Warrington.

At just over 30km and with a total climb of just over 10m, this is both the shortest, and flatest of the Beer Coaster stages.
