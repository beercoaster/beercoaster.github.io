An open ended personal challenge, that will really challenge you.

Beer Coaster consists of seven stages varying in distance between 34 and 42 kilometres that will take you coast to coast, brewtap to brewtap.

It can be done whichever direction you want, when you want. Whether you want to dip in and do a stage a year, or a month, or set out to do them all back to back!

There's no hard and fast rules, there's no fixed courses or routes, or even start and end places. Although we do have ones we recommend.

All we ask is that you take care, that you keep away from main roads, that you don't overstretch yourself, and that you have fun!

It's a perfect plan/opportunity for fundraising, whether you run or stroll it. It's possible to complete any of the stages as long as you're fit, healthy, and dedicated.
